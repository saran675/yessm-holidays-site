About Yessm Holidays

Yessm Holidays is an Indian travel brand built on a simple motto: everyone can travel. We craft practical, value‑packed trips with honest pricing and real human support from planning to touchdown.

Our Story

Started by Saran Pandiyan, Yessm grew from hands‑on travel across Asia—turning personal itineraries into reliable packages for families, couples, and teams.

Backpacked across the Philippines, Malaysia, Singapore, and Sri Lanka to understand their culture, food, logistics, and local rhythms—insights that shape every itinerary we build.

What We Stand For

  • Transparent pricing — clear inclusions, no surprise add‑ons.
  • Local authenticity — experiences led by verified local partners, not tourist traps.
  • Comfort with value — handpicked stays that balance quality, location, and budget.
  • Responsible travel — respect for culture, communities, and the environment.
  • Human support — 24×7 help via WhatsApp and phone throughout your trip.
  • Tailored planning — itineraries matched to your vibe (honeymoon, family, or corporate).
